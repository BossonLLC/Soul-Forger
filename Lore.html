In the beginning, Terranith was barren, devoid of life or purpose. But one day, six gods came from the heavens above, each wielding the power of one of the fundamental elements of the world, Fire, Water, Air, Earth, Light, and Dark. Together, they breathed life into the barren planet, filling it with forests, oceans, mountains, and creatures of all kinds. As time passed, intelligent beings emerged on Terranith, determined to make a name for themselves. They spent countless years learning how to harness the power of the elements, building marvelous structures and cities that stood the test of time. But just as the people of Terranith were beginning to master the art of crafting, a miraculous discovery was made. Ronum crystals, powerful gems said to have been created by the gods, found scattered throughout the world. Upon touching the crystals, people discovered that they could become so much more, imbued with the power of the elements and able to shape the world around them with their sheer will. With the discovery of the Ronum crystals, a new era dawned on Terranith. The dawn of Soul Forging had begun, and the people of the world set out to explore the vast reaches of their planet, forging their destinies and creating a world filled with wonder and magic.
